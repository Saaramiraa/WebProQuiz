Subang Bestari

[Subang Bestari, Selangor]
I've been living in Subang Bestari since I was in kindergarten, and I absolutely love it here! This lively little township in Shah Alam has everything you need and more. The location is super convenient, with easy access to highways that make getting to Kuala Lumpur and Subang Jaya a breeze.

What I really enjoy are the parks and open spaces—perfect for hanging out with friends or just relaxing outdoors, and it has a family-friendly vibe.

With all the shops and restaurants nearby, it’s hard to get bored! Subang Bestari isn’t just a place to live; it’s where I’ve made so many amazing memories and continue to have a great time every day!
